About Me

Hi, I’m Mariska, a teacher and passionate photographer, and one of the hearts behind I M Captured. I M Captured is created to tell stories through beautiful, authentic imagery. My background in teaching has shaped the way I connect with people — with patience, creativity, and heart. Every session is a chance to capture life’s moments with purpose and emotion, creating memories that last a lifetime.

Why choose I M Captured

At I M Captured, we believe every moment tells a story worth preserving. Our passion lies in capturing authentic emotion — the laughter, the details, and the in-between moments that make each occasion unique. Whether it’s a wedding, family shoot, event, or creative project, we combine artistic vision with professional precision to deliver timeless, high-quality images that truly reflect your story. With a focus on beauty, purpose, and personal connection, I M Captured isn’t just about taking photos — it’s about creating memories you’ll treasure forever.

What we capture

At I M Captured, we offer a wide range of photography services designed to bring your vision to life. From heartfelt weddings and family portraits to creative lifestyle and event photography, we aim to capture every moment with authenticity and artistic flair. We also work closely with businesses and brands, providing professional product, branding, and corporate photography that helps showcase their story, services, and identity in the best possible light.
